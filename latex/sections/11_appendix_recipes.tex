\section{Crafting Recipes}
\label{app:recipes}

Table~\ref{tab:recipes} lists the crafting recipe for every item provided by the mod. All
fifteen use only vanilla Minecraft ingredients, with no dependency on any other mod. Every
recipe listed is shapeless (order-independent) except the power supply, function generator,
probe, and X-Y probe, which require their ingredients arranged in the specific $3\times3$
(or, for the two probes, $1\times3$) pattern noted in the table.
